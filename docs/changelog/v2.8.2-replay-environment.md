# DELTA v2.8.2 — Replay Environment Assumptions Changelog Entry

Type: Documentation-only release

## Added

- Replay environment assumptions document.
- Proof of Replay boundary document.
- Determinism levels L0-L3.
- Distinction between `replay passed` and `replay verified`.
- Guidance for deterministic, best-effort, and manual-review replay.
- Future environment metadata recommendations.

## Purpose

This release documents the environmental assumptions behind DELTA Proof of Replay.

It clarifies that replay results must be interpreted together with:

- measurement method,
- method version,
- method hash,
- tool versions,
- dependency versions,
- operating system/runtime,
- replay instructions,
- environment declaration,
- determinism level,
- output comparison rules.

## Security

- Proof of Replay does not prove legal truth or real-world truth.
- Proof of Replay does not prove that the original environment was identical.
- Proof of Replay does not prove that all future machines will reproduce the result.
- Replay status is separated from trust status and revocation status.
- Missing or unverifiable environment metadata may require `manual_review`.
